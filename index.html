About

I am a Postdoc in the SFB “Higher invariants” at the University of Regensburg, working in the research group of Denis-Charles Cisinski and interested in everything (higher) category theory. I received my PhD in 2018 from the University of Regensburg under the supvervision of George Raptis and Ulrich Bunke.
